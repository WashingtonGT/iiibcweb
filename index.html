The EU AI Act's general-purpose AI model obligations took effect this week. If your organization uses any foundational model — GPT-4o, Claude, Gemini — via API, you now have implicit obligations under Article 53. Most legal teams are not aware. Your CAIO should brief counsel before Q3.

─── NOISE TO IGNORE ───────────────────────────

The "AI agent replacing entire departments" narrative accelerated again this week. Ignore it. Current agentic systems require more human oversight per task than they replace, not less. The value is in specific, bounded workflows — not organizational restructuring.

Audit which third-party AI APIs your engineering team is calling in production and determine whether any process personal data of EU residents. This is a 2-hour task that could prevent a significant compliance exposure.
